Laboratory & Facilities

My lab supports both experimental and computational mechanics. Available equipment includes:

  • Finite-element workstation running Abaqus 2026 (Intel i9, 64 GB RAM, 24 GB GPU)
  • Dantec 2D digital image correlation for full-field strain measurement
  • Hand lay-up and hot-press fabrication for composites and fiber-metal laminates
  • Sandblasting and electrochemical surface-etching rigs for FML bonding
  • Mitutoyo SJ-210 surface-roughness tester and Ossila contact-angle goniometer
  • Bambu Lab X1-Carbon FDM 3D printer and Elegoo laser engraver

Equipment is available for collaborative and student research — please contact me to arrange access.
