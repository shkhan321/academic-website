Biography

Dr. Sanan H. Khan is an Associate Professor of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at United Arab Emirates University. He completed his Ph.D. in Solid Mechanics and Design at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur in 2018, followed by postdoctoral research at IIT Delhi and a faculty post at Aligarh Muslim University before joining UAEU. His work sits where experiment meets simulation: he builds and tests composite and fiber-metal structures in the lab, then reproduces their behaviour with finite-element models. Recent projects span impact and ballistic damage in layered materials, shape-memory-alloy actuators for adaptive wings, 3D-printed and multi-material components, and the durability of Type-IV hydrogen storage vessels — much of it carried out alongside his graduate students.
