Brief Biography

Dr. Sanan H. Khan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at United Arab Emirates University. He received his Ph.D. in Solid Mechanics and Design from the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK) in 2018. Prior to joining UAEU, he held a postdoctoral position at IIT Delhi and an Assistant Professor position at Aligarh Muslim University. Dr. Khan’s research focuses on computational and experimental mechanics, with a particular emphasis on impact dynamics, composite materials, and the application of finite element methods to structural analysis. He has a strong publication record in leading international journals and actively participates in conferences.
