Biography

Dr. Sanan H. Khan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at United Arab Emirates University. He received his Ph.D. in Solid Mechanics and Design from the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK) in 2018. Prior to joining UAEU, he held a postdoctoral position at IIT Delhi and served as an Assistant Professor at Aligarh Muslim University. Dr. Khan's research focuses on computational and experimental mechanics, with a particular emphasis on impact dynamics, composite materials, and the application of finite element methods to structural analysis. He has a strong publication record in leading international journals and actively participates in conferences. His work is highly relevant to aerospace, defense, and automotive engineering. Dr. Khan has secured and managed research projects exceeding 1.5 million AED, including recent grants from Al Ain Municipality and a joint research project with Abu Dhabi University.
