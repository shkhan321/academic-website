Biography

Dr. Sanan H. Khan is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at United Arab Emirates University (UAEU). He received his Ph.D. in Solid Mechanics and Design from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur in 2018, and subsequently carried out postdoctoral research at IIT Delhi and held a faculty appointment at Aligarh Muslim University before joining UAEU.

His research bridges experiment and simulation: he designs, fabricates, and tests composite and fiber-metal laminate structures in the laboratory and models their behaviour using finite-element analysis. His interests include impact and progressive damage in layered materials, ballistic protection, shape-memory-alloy actuation for adaptive aircraft wings, additive and multi-material manufacturing, and the durability of Type-IV hydrogen storage vessels, increasingly supported by AI/ML methods for design and optimisation.

Dr. Khan has authored over 50 peer-reviewed journal and conference papers (h-index 16) and leads several funded projects as Principal Investigator, together exceeding AED 1.2 million, with partners including Boeing, Al Ain Municipality, and UAEU. He teaches undergraduate and graduate courses in mechanics, finite-element methods, and aerospace structures, and supervises BSc, MSc, and PhD students in his research group.
