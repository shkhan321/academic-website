Biography

Dr. Sanan H. Khan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at UAE University. He received his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK) in Solid Mechanics and Design. His research interests include computational and experimental mechanics, failure in composites, and damage modeling.

Research Interests

  • Failure analysis of fiber metal laminates
  • Computational mechanics of composite materials
  • Mesh-objective damage modeling
  • VUMAT implementation for progressive failure

Selected Publications
