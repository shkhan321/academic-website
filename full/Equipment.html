Laboratory Equipment

Tools for Advanced Materials Research and Fabrication

Lab equipment is available for collaborative and student research. To arrange access, contact Dr. Khan at shkhan@uaeu.ac.ae.

Finite Element Analysis: Workstation with Abaqus 2026

Purpose: high-performance workstation (Intel i9, 64 GB RAM, 24 GB GPU) running Abaqus CAE 2026 for finite-element simulation.

Applications: nonlinear and progressive-damage analysis of composite and fiber-metal laminate structures.

DIC Setup

Purpose: Dantec 2D Digital Image Correlation system for non-contact, full-field strain measurement during mechanical testing.

Applications: tracking deformation on composite and FML specimens; paired with a 100 kN MTS universal testing machine.

Dino-Lite Digital Macroscope

Purpose: high-resolution macro-imaging for surface inspection and damage characterisation.

Applications: documenting delamination, matrix cracking, fiber pull-out, and impact damage in composites.

Hand Lay-up Method

Purpose: fabrication of FRP composites and fiber-metal laminates with controlled fiber orientation and stacking sequence.

Applications: rollers, brushes, and cutting tools, with a hot press for controlled curing.

Sandblasting Equipment

Purpose: creates controlled mechanical surface roughness on metal plates for fiber-metal-laminate fabrication.

Applications: roughness tuned via nozzle size, air pressure, and standoff distance.

Electrochemical Surface Etching

Purpose: in-house setup for controlled electrochemical roughening of metal surfaces.

Applications: roughness tuned via voltage (up to 12 V), temperature, and electrolyte for improved FML bonding.

Mitutoyo Surface Roughness Tester

Purpose: portable SJ-210 tester for precise surface-texture measurement (Ra, Rz, and related parameters).

Applications: quality control of mechanically and chemically treated surfaces in FML and composite work.

Contact Angle Goniometer (Ossila)

Purpose: measures wettability and surface energy from the contact angle of a liquid droplet.

Applications: assessing hydrophobicity, coatings, and surface treatments in materials research.

Ultrasonic Cleaner

Purpose: ultrasonic cleaning of specimens up to 200 × 200 mm.

Applications: surface preparation of metal plates before FML fabrication.

Bambu Lab X1-Carbon 3D Printer (AMS)

Purpose: high-precision FDM 3D printer with an Automatic Material System for multi-material and multi-colour printing.

Applications: rapid prototyping and functional engineering components.

Elegoo Laser Engraver

Purpose: 10 W laser engraver with a 400 × 400 mm bed for engraving and light cutting.

Applications: wood, leather, and acrylic; prototyping, labelling, and custom fabrication (LightBurn).
