Research Interests

  • Composite and fiber-metal laminate design, testing, and failure analysis
  • Impact, damage, and ballistic protection of layered materials
  • Finite-element and progressive-damage modelling (Abaqus)
  • Additive manufacturing and multi-material components
  • Smart structures and shape-memory-alloy actuation
  • AI / ML for engineering design and optimisation

Current Work

My current work spans impact and progressive-damage modelling of composite and fiber-metal structures, flexural failure of asymmetric CFRP laminates, shape-memory-alloy actuation for smart wings, and CFD/AI optimisation of UAV airfoils — combining laboratory testing with finite-element simulation. I welcome collaborations and student projects in these areas; please see the contact page.
