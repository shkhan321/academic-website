Current Research

My current research focuses on computational solid mechanics, with an emphasis on the impact dynamics of composite materials. We utilize advanced finite element methods (FEM) to simulate and analyze material behavior under high-velocity impact scenarios. The goal is to understand failure mechanisms, such as delamination and plug formation, to design more resilient and lightweight structures for aerospace and defense applications.
