Laboratory & Facilities

My lab supports both experimental and computational mechanics. Equipment is available for collaborative and student research — please contact me to arrange access.

Finite-Element Analysis Workstation (Abaqus 2026)

High-performance workstation (Intel i9, 64 GB RAM, 24 GB GPU) running Abaqus CAE 2026 for nonlinear and progressive-damage analysis of composite and fiber-metal structures.

Digital Image Correlation (DIC)

Dantec 2D DIC for non-contact, full-field strain measurement during mechanical testing, paired with a 100 kN MTS universal testing machine.

Dino-Lite Digital Macroscope

High-resolution macro-imaging for surface inspection and damage characterisation — delamination, matrix cracking, fiber pull-out, and impact damage.

Hand Lay-up Station

Fabrication of FRP composites and fiber-metal laminates with controlled fiber orientation and stacking, with a hot press for controlled curing.

Sandblasting Equipment

Controlled mechanical roughening of metal plates for fiber-metal-laminate fabrication; roughness tuned by nozzle, pressure, and standoff distance.

Electrochemical Surface Etching

In-house setup for controlled electrochemical roughening of metal surfaces to improve fiber-metal-laminate bonding.

Surface Roughness Tester (Mitutoyo SJ-210)

Portable, precise measurement of surface texture (Ra, Rz) for quality control of mechanically and chemically treated surfaces.

Contact-Angle Goniometer (Ossila)

Measures wettability and surface energy from a droplet's contact angle — assessing hydrophobicity, coatings, and surface treatments.

Ultrasonic Cleaner

Ultrasonic cleaning of specimens up to 200 × 200 mm for surface preparation of metal plates before FML fabrication.

Bambu Lab X1-Carbon 3D Printer (AMS)

High-precision FDM printer with an Automatic Material System for multi-material, multi-colour printing of functional prototypes.

Elegoo Laser Engraver

10 W laser engraver with a 400 × 400 mm bed for engraving and light cutting of wood, acrylic, and leather.
